India's Booming Power Sector

India is witnessing a remarkable transformation in its power sector. Last year marked a historic achievement as the country became the fourth-largest market globally in cumulative capacity and annual additions. With 5,400 MW of new wind power added, India's total capacity reached 32,380 MW by March 2017. This rapid growth underscores the nation's commitment to sustainable energy and positions India as a global leader in wind energy.
